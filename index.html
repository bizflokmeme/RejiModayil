Report Conclusion
  Campaign Summary

Campaign Delivered Outstanding Awareness Results

The Instagram awareness campaign for Reji Modiyil — founder of Hostao & Autochat — exceeded expectations, successfully achieving its primary objectives of expanding personal brand visibility and driving strong engagement across a highly targeted Malayalam-speaking audience. With a total reach of 508,000 unique accounts and an average engagement rate of 8.1% — significantly above the industry benchmark of 3–5% — the campaign is a clear testament to the power of authentic, community-driven content.

The 11 partnered pages played a pivotal role in amplifying Reji's story and his entrepreneurial journey, collectively generating over 350,000 impressions, 150,900 story views, and 8,841 likes. The campaign also received 30 comments, reflecting genuine audience conversations around Reji and his startups.

These results demonstrate that Reji Modiyil's story resonated deeply with the community. We strongly recommend building on this momentum with a conversion-focused follow-up campaign to translate this broad awareness into leads, sign-ups, and measurable growth for Hostao and Autochat.
